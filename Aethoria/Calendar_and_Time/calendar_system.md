# Aethorian Calendar System

## Basic Structure

### Time Units
- Day: 26 hours
- Week: 7 days
- Month: 28 days
- Year: 402 days (14.35 months)

### Standardized Calendar
- 14 full months
- 1 special period (Balance Week)
- 4 seasons
- 2 force cycles
